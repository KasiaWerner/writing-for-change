The 411

The goal of the course is to help you accomplish social change through op-eds, grants, applications and proposals. This course is for anyone who needs to write in order to accomplish social good in their communities. If you’re familiar with organizations like Public Allies, the Centre for Social Innovation, Summer Search or the Open Coalition--non-profits or social enterprises that have a passion to change the world--then this is your crew. At the end of the course, you’ll have a finished project that’s been polished by a crew of like-minded folks. Featured writers include Sarah Mirk of Bitch Media, Alexandrina Agloro of the University of Southern California and more.
